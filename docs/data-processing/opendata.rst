Open data
=========

A topic-based overview of public repositories containing research data.

Agricultural sciences
---------------------

`AQUASTAT Dissemination System <https://data.apps.fao.org/aquastat/?lang=en>`_
    Global information system of the *Food and Agriculture Organization of the
    United Nations* (FAO) on water resources and agricultural water management
`Data Commons Agriculture <https://datacommons.org/data/agriculture>`_
    Data Commons brings together public data from many parts of the world,
    including surveys
`FoodData Central <https://fdc.nal.usda.gov>`_
    Food composition data from the United States Department of Agriculture
    (USDA)
`Hyperspectral benchmark dataset on soil moisture <https://github.com/felixriese/hyperspectral-soilmoisture-dataset/>`_
    Hyperspectral and soil moisture data from a lysimeter field campaign based
    on a soil sample. Karlsruhe (Germany), 2017
`Index DataBase <https://www.indexdatabase.de/>`_
    Vegetation indices from the Institute of Crop Science and Resource
    Conservation (INRES)
`PLANTS Database <https://plants.sc.egov.usda.gov>`_
    Standardised information on vascular plants, mosses and lichens in the USA

Biology
-------

`American-Gut <https://github.com/biocore/American-Gut>`_
    Open-access code and IPython notebooks from the `American Gut
    <https://microsetta.ucsd.edu/american-gut-project/>`_ project
`ArrayExpress - Functional Genomics Data <https://www.ebi.ac.uk/biostudies/arrayexpress>`_
    Data from functional genomics experiments
`Catalogue of Life (COL) <https://www.catalogueoflife.org/data/download>`_
    Integrated list of all known species worldwide
`Cell Image Library (CIL) <https://www.cellimagelibrary.org/home>`_
    Over 12,000 datasets from the *Centre for Research in Biological Systems*
    (CRBS)
`CytoImageNet <https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/stanleyhua/cytoimagenet>`_
    Extensive dataset for pre-training with microscopy images
`Electron Microscopy Data Bank (EMDB) <https://www.ebi.ac.uk/emdb/>`_
    Data from cryo-electron microscopy (cryo-EM) and representative tomograms of
    macromolecular complexes and subcellular structures
`EMBL-EBI <https://www.ebi.ac.uk/services/>`_
    Data sources and analysis tools from the European Bioinformatics Institute
    of the European Molecular Biology Laboratory (EMBL)
`ENCODE portal <https://www.encodeproject.org>`_
    The :abbr:`ENCODE (Encyclopedia of DNA Elements)` Consortium is an ongoing
    international collaborative project involving research groups, funded by the
    `National Human Genome Research Institute (NHGRI)
    <https://www.genome.gov/>`_
`EnsemblGenomes <https://ensemblgenomes.org>`_
    Genomic data for invertebrate species, as well as tools for processing,
    analysing and visualising this data
`FireBrowse portal <http://firebrowse.org>`_
    FireBrowse provides access to a wide range of cancer genomics data,
    including clinical annotations, DNA copy number, miR, miRseq, mRNA and
    mRNAseq
`Gene Expression Omnibus <https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/geo/>`_
    Functional genomics data supporting the submission of `MIAME
    <https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/11726920/>`_-compliant data
`Gene Ontology <https://geneontology.org>`_
    Gene Ontology (GO) knowledge base on gene functions
`Genomic Data Commons Data Portal <https://gdc.cancer.gov/access-data/gdc-data-portal>`_
    Data from genomic cancer studies
`Global Biotic Interactions (GloBI) <https://www.globalbioticinteractions.org/data>`_
    Data on species interactions, such as predator-prey, pollinator-plant,
    pathogen-host and parasite-host
`ICOS PSP benchmarks <https://ico2s.org/datasets/psp_benchmark.html>`_
    A collection of practical benchmarks designed to test the scalability of
    classification and regression methods developed by the :abbr:`ICOS
    (Interdisciplinary Computing and Complex BioSystems)` research group
`IGSR: The International Genome Sample Resource <https://www.internationalgenome.org/>`_
    As part of the ‘1000 Genomes’ project, a catalogue of common genetic
    variations in humans was compiled
`Journal of Cell Biology <https://www.ebi.ac.uk/biostudies/JCB/studies>`_
    A collection of image data relating to articles published in the ‘Journal of
    Cell Biology’.
`KEGG: Kyoto Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes <https://www.genome.jp/kegg/>`_
    Database on higher-level functions and relationships within biological
    systems such as cells, organisms, ecosystems and the biosphere, based on
    information at the molecular level
`NIH Human Microbiome Project Catalog <https://www.hmpdacc.org/hmp/hmp/catalog/>`_
    Metadata on all reference genomes of human-related isolates and on
    metagenome samples from healthy humans
`National Center for Biotechnology Information <https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/guide/all/>`_
    Databases, including those on chemicals and bioassays, DNA and RNA, and
    homology
`openSNP <https://www.openhumans.org/activity/opensnp/>`_
    Repository for genetic and phenotypic data
`palmerpenguins <https://allisonhorst.github.io/palmerpenguins/>`_
    Dataset for data exploration and visualisation as an alternative to Iris
`Pathguide <https://pathguide.org/>`_
    Resources on biological signalling pathways and molecular interactions
`RCSB Protein Data Bank (RCSB PDB) <https://www.rcsb.org>`_
    Data on experimentally determined 3D structures, integrative 3D structures
    and computer-generated structural models (CSM)
`Personal Genome Project <https://www.personalgenomes.org>`_
    Publicly accessible data on genomes, health and traits
`PGC Data Access Portals <https://pgc.unc.edu/for-researchers/data-access-committee/data-access-portal/>`_
    Portals for querying data at the individual level or with restricted access
`Rfam <https://rfam.org>`_
    Collection of RNA families, each represented by multiple sequence alignment
    (MSA), consensus RNA structures and covariance models
`SSBD:database <https://ssbd.riken.jp/database/>`_
    Open resources for the analysis of microscopic images and quantitative data
    of biological objects, such as single molecules, cells, tissues,
    individuals, :abbr:`etc. (et cetera)`
`UniGene <https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/HomoloGene/HTML/UniGene.html>`_
    NCBI database on the transcriptome and therefore not primarily a database
    for genes
`UniProt <https://www.uniprot.org/help/downloads>`_
    Freely accessible source of protein sequences and functional information
`UCSC Genome Browser <https://hgdownload.gi.ucsc.edu/downloads.html>`_
    Sequence and annotation data for the genome sequences displayed in the UCSC
    Genome Browser

Chemistry
---------

`Ionic Liquids Database - ILThermo <https://ilthermo.boulder.nist.gov>`_
    Online search tool for the thermodynamic and transport properties of ionic
    liquids, as well as binary and ternary mixtures containing ionic liquids
`PubChem <https://pubchem.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov>`_
    A collection of freely accessible chemical information from the National
    Center for Biotechnology Information

Climate and Weather
-------------------

    aviation

    Monthly and seasonal data by region and component
`Average city temperatures <https://www.fetchseries.com/climate/average-city-temperatures-fsr/>`_
    Daily data on average air temperatures in major cities worldwide

    Historical data by station name, province, territory or distance
`Caravan <https://zenodo.org/records/7540792#.Y-QQGK3MKUn>`_
    Global hydrology dataset with large samples
`CDC – Climate Data Center <https://cdc.dwd.de/portal/>`_
    Climate data from the german weather service (DWD)
`Climate Data Online (CDO) <https://www.bom.gov.au/climate/data/index.shtml>`_
    Statistics, current weather observations and climate data from the
    Australian Data Archive for Meteorology (ADAM)
`Climatic Research Unit <https://www.uea.ac.uk/groups-and-centres/climatic-research-unit/data>`_
    Data provided by the CRU of the National Centre for Atmospheric Science
    (NCAS)
`Copernicus Climate Change Service (C3S) <https://cds.climate.copernicus.eu>`_

    Copernicus programme
`European Climate Assessment & Dataset (ECA&D) <https://www.ecad.eu>`_

`GDELT Project: Four Massive Datasets Charting The Global Climate Change News Narrative 2009-2020 <https://blog.gdeltproject.org/four-massive-datasets-charting-the-global-climate-change-news-narrative-2009-2020/>`_

    2009 to 2020
`NOAA Global Radiation and Aerosols (GRAD) Data <https://gml.noaa.gov/grad/>`_
    Long-term measurements of radiation, meteorological parameters and aerosols
    at various remote locations worldwide, as well as at sites across the
    Americas
`NOAA Local Climatological Data (LCD) <https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/metadata/geoportal/rest/metadata/item/gov.noaa.ncdc:C01689/html>`_
    Summaries of climatological conditions from airports and other major weather
    stations
`Open-Meteo <https://open-meteo.com>`_
    Open data with a high resolution of 1 to 11 kilometres
`WorldClim <https://www.worldclim.org>`_
    Maps, graphs, tables and data on the global climate

Energy sector
-------------

`Almanac of Minutely Power dataset (AMPds) <http://ampds.org>`_
    Two years’ worth of minute-by-minute measurement data on electricity, water
    and natural gas
`Commercial Building Energy Dataset (COMBED) <https://combed.github.io>`_
    Energy-related dataset from a commercial building, with data recorded more
    than once per minute
`Direct Borohydride Fuel Cell (DBFC) Dataset <https://github.com/ECSIM/dbfc-dataset>`_
    Impedance and polarisation measurements at the anode using Pd/C, Pt/C and
    Pd-coated Ni–Co/rGO catalysts
`Domestic Electrical Load Survey (DELS) Secure Data 1994–2014 <https://www.datafirst.uct.ac.za/dataportal/index.php/catalog/757>`_
    The ‘DELS Secure Data’ dataset contains anonymised survey responses
`ECO data set (Electricity Consumption & Occupancy) <https://vs.inf.ethz.ch/res/show.html?what=eco-data>`_
    Non-intrusive load monitoring and occupancy detection over an eight-month
    period in six Swiss households
`EIA-923 <https://www.eia.gov/electricity/data/eia923/>`_
    The EIA-923 questionnaire collects detailed electricity data on power
    generation, fuel consumption, fossil fuel stocks and goods received at the
    level of power stations and generating units
`Global Power Plant Database <https://datasets.wri.org/datasets/global-power-plant-database>`_
    Global open-source database for power stations
`Household Electricity Study - EV0702 <https://randd.defra.gov.uk/ProjectDetails?ProjectID=17359&FromSearch=Y&Publisher=1&SearchText=EV0702&SortString=ProjectCode&SortOrder=Asc&Paging=10#Description>`_
    Data on household electricity consumption from April 2010 to April 2011 from
    domestic appliances in a total of 251 owner-occupied households across
    England
`High Frequency EMI Data Set (HFED) <https://hfed.github.io>`_
    Data set on high-frequency electromagnetic interference (EMI) containing
    measurement curves derived from a signal analyser and a Universal Software
    Radio Peripheral (USRP)
`Moroccan buildings’ electricity consumption dataset (MORED) <https://github.com/MOREDataset/MORED>`_
    Data on the electricity consumption of various urban buildings in Moroccan
    cities
`Marktstammdatenregister (MaStR) <https://www.marktstammdatenregister.de/MaStR/Datendownload>`_
    Basic data on the electricity and gas market
`Proton Exchange Membrane (PEM) Fuel Cell Dataset <https://github.com/ECSIM/pem-dataset1>`_
    Standard tests on Nafion-112 membranes and MEA activation tests of a PEM
    fuel cell under various operating conditions
`Plug Load Appliance Identification Dataset (PLAID) <https://nicholasinstitute.duke.edu/energy-data-resources/plug-load-appliance-identification-dataset-plaid>`_
    Voltage and current measurements at a sampling rate of 30 kHz on 11
    different appliance types in more than 60 households in Pittsburgh,
    Pennsylvania
`Public Utility Data Liberation Project (PUDL) <https://github.com/catalyst-cooperative/pudl>`_
    Open-source data processing pipeline that facilitates access to US energy
    data and its programmatic use
`Smart Meter Data Listing <https://github.com/RSLi/smart-meter-data-catalog>`_
    List of datasets relating to smart meters
`SynD <https://www.nature.com/articles/s41597-020-0434-6>`_
    Synthetic energy dataset for non-intrusive load monitoring in households
    (SynD) provides a synthetic energy dataset with a focus on residential
    buildings
`tracebase data set <https://github.com/areinhardt/tracebase>`_
    Collection of electricity consumption data for research purposes in the
    field of energy analysis
`UK Domestic Appliance-Level Electricity (UK-DALE) dataset <https://jack-kelly.com/data/>`_
    Electricity demand from five homes and individual appliances recorded every
    six seconds
`Indian Dataset for Ambient Water and Energy <https://iawe.github.io>`_
    Energy monitoring and energy consumption of a home in India over 73 days
